Dynamic British Sign Language Recognition on Edge Device

Image taken by Me
Status: Ongoing Python Embedded Linux Tensorflow OpenCV Project Management Embedded Machine Learning CUDA
During a collaboration meeting with a deaf speaker and my TEDx colleague in the 2018 TEDx Sunway University Talk, I observed the challenges when the interpreter needed a bathroom break. This made me think:
"What if we could communicate instantly with technology, ensuring seamless interaction at all times?"

Implementing video British Sign Language recognition of up to 20 words on Nvidia Jetson Nano. Project includes the comparison of different feature extraction techniques and machine learning models.

Optimization to discover includes: Tensorflow RT, CUDA, Overclocking

This is an ongoing project - I will provide more details after completion
