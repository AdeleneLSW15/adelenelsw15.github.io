Nemui Store - CoFounder

Photo taken by me | Logo Design by my team
 Shopify Marketing Email Automation Entrepreneurship

‘Nemui’ stems from the Japanese word meaning ‘sleep’. Believing that everyone deserves a good rest, it offers a variety of innovative and minimalistic products to help people achieve a high quality of sleep.

Work Done

  • Established a compelling online e-commerce platform on Shopify, featuring high-quality product imagery.
  • Executed targeted online advertising campaigns across social media platforms, like Instagram and Facebook, leveraging optimal internet traffic statistics from Instagram.
  • Analysis of marketing data, identifying top-performing products and adjusting strategies for conversion rate.
  • Researched and evaluated available products in the market, placing orders to physically assess them to ensure quality standards.
  • Implemented blog marketing strategies for Nemui products, and also contributed to the copywriting for the Nemui store website.
  • Product photography for Nemui store products, taking product photography experience gained A Vintage Collezione Sdn Bhd.

    • Learnings and Reflection

    Initiated during the challenges of the COVID era, Nemui Store faced discontinuation due to co-founders dispersing to different global universities. However, the thrill of acquiring the first customer was an indescribable feeling. This journey cultivated valuable soft skills and honed my ability to strategize product positioning in the market.
